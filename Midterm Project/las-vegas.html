01 Sphere

The Sphere in Las Vegas is a cutting-edge entertainment venue known for its massive LED exterior that displays stunning visuals visible across the city. Inside, it offers an immersive experience with advanced sound and visual technology that redefines live performances. The venue’s interior features state-of-the-art spatial audio and 4D effects, allowing audiences to feel completely surrounded by the show, whether it’s a concert, a theatrical performance, or a multimedia presentation. Its innovative design creates a sense of being inside a living, breathing digital world, making every event unforgettable.

02 Coca-Cola Store

The Coca-Cola Store in Las Vegas is a vibrant, multi-level shop where visitors can explore exclusive Coca-Cola merchandise and try drinks from around the world. It’s a fun and lively spot on the Strip that celebrates the brand’s history and global flavors. Visitors can sample over a dozen international sodas at the “Taste of the World” experience, shop for unique Coca-Cola souvenirs, and even take photos with the giant Coca-Cola bottle outside. The store’s bright colors, upbeat music, and nostalgic displays make it a must-visit place for fans of the brand and anyone looking for a cheerful Las Vegas experience.

03 Bellagio

The Bellagio Hotel in Las Vegas is renowned for its elegant architecture and the stunning Conservatory & Botanical Gardens, which transform with the seasons. Inside, vibrant flowers, plants, and artistic displays create a breathtaking natural spectacle, carefully curated by horticulturists to reflect seasonal themes, holidays, and cultural celebrations. The hotel also features the iconic Fountains of Bellagio, a mesmerizing water and light show choreographed to music, as well as fine art at the Bellagio Gallery, world-class dining, and luxury shopping. Combining nature, art, and sophistication, the Bellagio offers visitors a truly immersive and unforgettable experience.

04 Food

In Las Vegas, I tried a variety of delicious foods from different restaurants. From street snacks to fine dining, every meal was a new and exciting experience. I sampled savory street tacos bursting with flavor, sweet and creamy desserts from local bakeries, and indulgent dishes at high-end restaurants on the Strip. Each place offered something unique, from exotic international cuisines to modern takes on classic American favorites. The vibrant food scene in Las Vegas not only satisfied my hunger but also introduced me to creative culinary combinations and flavors I had never tried before, making every meal a memorable adventure.
